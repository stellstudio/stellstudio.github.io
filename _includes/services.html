How It Works



My name is Nathan Stell, and I'm a violin teacher.
Whether remote or local, it's my mission to keep lessons simple, effective, and accessible.

Unlimited Access to Instructor

The set monthly price guarantees you at least 4 lessons within the month. In addition to that, if you feel you would like and can handle more instruction, just visit the online calendar and book more time in one of the available time slots.
You'll also be able to text, call, or email me if you have any questions in between lesson times.

Extended Lesson Time

Lesson times often vary based on the age and skill level of the student. For students 6 years of age and younger, at least two 15 minute lessons per week is recommended.

Weekly Check-In Option

This is a time, once a week, to discuss possible problems, celebrate break-throughs, and even brainstorm ideas. It can be done face-to-face online, or over the phone.
It only takes 5-10 minutes and is completely optional.
