How it Works

Whether remote or local, lessons are kept simple and accessible.

Before We Start

Contact me and we'll discuss a platform (Skype, Zoom, Facetime, etc...). We'll also talk about music goals and how to build the best environment for learning the violin, tailored to your needs. I'll also give you access to the online calendar where you will be able to sign up for lesson times throughout the week.

Lessons

Lessons will be a combination of technique exercises, supplementary games/activities, and stong study. Lesson times vary and will depend on the age and skill level of the student. For children below the age of 7, shorter and more frequent lessons are recommended.

Check-Ins

All students and/or parents will have an opportunity to meet with me apart from their lesson(s) during the week. It is a chance for me to help with any possible problems, celebrate break-throughs, and answer any questions you may have. It is completely optional but can be highly beneficial.
