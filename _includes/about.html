About Me

Get to know Nathan Stell

  • 1985

    A Young Start

    Began playing a violin at the age of three under a Suzuki Violin Instructor in El Paso, TX.

  • 2000

    Higher Education

    Continued studying the violin throughout elementary, middle, and high school, and pursued music in university. Played 2nd chair, 1st violin in the UTEP Symphony Orchestra and 5th chair, 1st violin in the University of Notre Dame Symphony Orchestra.

  • Aug 2001

    Private Lessons

    Began teaching private violin lessons to local middle and high school students.

  • 2008 - Present

    Public Lessons

    Hired to be director of orchestra, band, and choir for three different elementary/middle schools.

  • August 2014

    The Birth of an Idea

    Conceived a new way to teach lessons and interact with students and parents.
