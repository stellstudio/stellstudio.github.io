About Me

Get to Know Nathan Stell

  • 1985

    A Young Start

    Began playing violin at the age of three under a Suzuki Violin Instructor in El Paso, TX.

  • 2000

    Higher Education

    Pursued music and theatre in college. Played 2nd chair, 1st violin in the UTEP Symphony Orchestra and 5th chair, 1st violin in the University of Notre Dame Symphony Orchestra.

  • 2001

    Giving Back

    Began teaching private violin lessons to local middle and high school students.

  • 2008 - Present

    In the Schools

    Served as the music director of orchestra, band, and choir for three different private schools.

  • August 2014

    The Birth of an Era

    Conceived a new way to teach lessons and interact with students and parents.
